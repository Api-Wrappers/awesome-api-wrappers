# Code of Conduct

This is a curated list. Most interaction happens through issues and pull requests — but since people do discuss entries, debate inclusion decisions, and review each other's submissions, a few ground rules make sense.

## What we expect

- Keep feedback about the work, not the person
- If you disagree with an inclusion decision, explain your reasoning — vague objections or personal attacks go nowhere
- Assume good faith unless there's a real reason not to

## What we don't tolerate

- Harassment in any form — in issues, pull requests, comments, or anywhere else connected to this project
- Discriminatory language or behavior based on who someone is
- Threatening, demeaning, or deliberately hostile conduct

## Enforcement

If something happens that shouldn't, open an issue or contact the maintainers directly. Reports will be taken seriously and handled promptly.
